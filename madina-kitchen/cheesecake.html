Raspberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • For the base:
  • 1 1/4cup biscuits 270g
  • 1/2 butter 90g
  • For the filling:
  • 4 cups cream cheese 900g
  • 3/4 cup whipping cream 200ml
  • 1 cup sugar 250g, 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3eggs + 1 egg yolk
  • juice of half lemon, 3tbsp flour
  • For the raspberry coulis:
  • 1 1/2 cup raspberry 250 g
  • 1/3 cup sugar 75g
  • Juice of laf lemon

Instructions

  1. Place the biscuit into a food processor, pulse until fine crumbs form, then add in the melted butter.Transfer the crumbs into the bottom of a 9-inch (23-cm) spring form pan and press them firmly into place.
  2. Make the batter just like a classic cheesecake – with cream cheese, sugar, eggs, vanilla and lemon juice.Beat the cream cheese all by itself for at least 8 to 10 minutes until it’s smooth and creamy.
  3. Preheat oven to 400F. Bake the cheesecake at 400F for 10 minutes, then lower to 375F and bake for 40 minutes. The middle will be slightly wobbly, and the sides will be more firmly set.
  4. Allow the cheesecake to cool down slowly for a few hours down to room temperature. Place the cheesecake into the refrigerator to cool overnight, or for at least 6 hours.
