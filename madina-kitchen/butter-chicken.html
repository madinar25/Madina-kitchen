Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • To marinate chicken
  • 200g chicken, 1 tsp ginger&garlic
  • Hung curd-2 tsp, Lemon juice- 1/2 lemon
  • Chili Powder-1 tsp, Garam Masala- 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp, Oil-1 tbsp
  • To make masala paste
  • Butter-1 tbsp, Oil-1 tbsp, Onion- 2 chopped
  • 1tsp ginger&garlic, Tomato-3 chopped
  • Chili powder-1 tsp, Salt-1 tsp, Cumin powder-1 tsp
  • Coriander powder-1/2 tsp, Garam Masala-1 tsp
  • Water-1/2 cup, Cashew nuts-10,
  • To make butter chicken
  • Butter-2 tbsp, Chili powder-1 tsp, Cream-1/4cup

Instructions

  1. To a bowl, add the boneless chicken pieces, pound ginger garlic, hung curd, lemon juice, red chili powder, garam masala and salt. Mix everything well, and marinate the chicken for about 30 minutes
  2. Heat a pan and add oil. Fry the marinated chicken pieces mixture till they are cooked and keep it aside. In a pan heat oil and butter, add onions and saute them till they are transparent . Add the pound ginger garlic and mix it well. Then add tomatoes saute them.
  3. Add kashmiri red chilies and saute. Add salt, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder. Add some water and cook till the tomatoes are mushy. Then add cashewnuts and saute for 5 mins. Cool down the cooked masala and grind it to a fine paste by adding water.
  4. Heat the same pan, add butter, red chili powder and keep the flame on low. Add the ground puree to the pan. Saute and add water. Add the fried chicken pieces and mix it well. Add fresh cream mix it and simmer the gravy for about 10 minutes by closing the kadai.
  5. Finally, add crushed kasuri methi and coriander leaves and mix well.Serve the Butter Chicken hot with naan, roti or pulao.
