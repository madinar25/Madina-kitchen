Tiramisu (Tatami)

Ingredients:

  • 100g instant pudding
  • 400g milk
  • 320g mascapone
  • 780g whipped cream
  • 125g sugar
  • 8 shots of espresso
  • Tate's chocolate cookies
  • Cocao powder

Instructions

  1. Blend the instant pudding and milk, let it chill in the fridge.
  2. Mix the mascarpone with whipped cream using mixer, adding sugar slowly to thicken.
  3. Fold the pudding with the prepared cream
  4. Dip the cookies in the espresso by layering cookies then cream
  5. Top it off with cocao powder
